Need Help?

Well, you should be able to see 4 GUI sections in the app.

At the bottom right corner,
You can click the "Next" button to go to a new random location;
You can click the "Previous" button to go to the previously viewed location;
You can click the "Region: ..." button to select the region that you want to go;
You can click the "Roam" button to view different 360 images at the same city.

At the bottom left corner,
you can find the city and country names of the location;
You can click the "More Info" button to see more information about the location;
You can click the "BOOK A VACATION!" button to go to the corresponding Expedia sale page to find some great travel packages related to the location.

At the top right corner,
you can find an embedded Google Map that displays the geographic information of the location.
